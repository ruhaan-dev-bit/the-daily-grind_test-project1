Roaster's Secrets

Expand your knowledge of the brew while you enjoy your warm, comfortable table workspace.

The "Bean" Myth

Coffee beans are actually seeds! They are nestled inside thick, sweet cherries that grow on woody highland shrubs. When we roast them, they develop the delicious caramel flavors we love.

Aroma Healing

Smelling roasted coffee directly triggers a change in your brain's protein structures, lowering tension levels. It acts as an instant relaxer before you take your first sip of the day.

The Snack Ball Past

Before drinking coffee as a beverage, early African tribes combined raw crushed coffee seeds with animal fats to roll up quick-energy snack bites to fuel them on journeys.
